Course Overview

The 18-hole "Leland" course at the Leland Country Club facility in Leland, Michigan features 5,867 yards of golf from the longest tees for a par of 71. The course rating is 69.0 and it has a slope rating of 123. Designed by Charles B. Wilder/C.D. Wagstaff, the Leland golf course opened in 1914.
